Add new account info

To add new account info you need to click on the account menu. After that the following screen will come

Now you need to enter your your name and enter your password and other informations and click on save button. The information will be saved and added at the table. You can generate the password instead of enter password. To generate password you can select the check box parameters such as numeric characters, Uppercase Letters, Lowercase Letters and Special characters.

Edit account info

To edit account info you need to click table row which you want to edit. The info will be displayed at the entry panel and the update button will be enabled. You can update your information and click on the update button to save the updated information.

Delete account info

To delete account info you need to click table row which you want to delete. The info will be displayed at the entry panel and the delete button will be enabled. You can delete your information by clicking on the delete button.
